Rooms & Suites

Hotel Myanmar has 157 elegant guest rooms and suites, all with heart-stopping views of the lake or the charming scenery. Unique room designs and layouts characterize our historic hotel.The hotel is around a 20-minute drive from Yangon International Airport and offers an airport shuttle service. The helpful staff at the tour desk are available to book tours and sightseeing trips in Yangon.

Welcome to Hotel Myanmar

Step into the past and experience the splendor of the Hotel Myanmar at 129 Dhammazedi Road, Yangon (Rangoon), Myanmar, the one of hotels situated nearest to the ShweDagon Pagoda. Start your vacation in Yangon and experience the allure of old world charm at one of the best myamar hotels, where tasteful, modern conveniences blend with the warmth of authentic Burmese style. Vibrant decor and authentic Burmese architecture intermingle with indulgent amenities including complimentary Wi-Fi, concierge services, free water, Nespresso machine, complimentary art & history tours 4 days a week, a business center and much more. Each of our 180 beautifully appointed guest rooms and suites present an opportunity to experience the elegance of our property, boasting original artwork and hand-crafted furniture and luxury amenities. An authentic example of one of 4-Diamond hotels, Hotel Myanmar offers classic burmense dining experiences including a one-of-a-kind, seasonal, outdoor traditional dinner . Breathtaking event venues showcase views of the ShweDagon pagoda, creating an idyllic location for weddings and events while stately meeting rooms and a complimentary business center make Hotel Myanmar the ideal solution for your business meetings and corporate travel. Our central location on the Yangon city provides guests with unsurpassed access to original local artwork, entertainment and attractions.
